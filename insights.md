# Rental Market Insights: SAS Nagar & Surrounding Areas

### 1. Supply & Configuration
* **2 BHK Dominance:** The market is heavily saturated with 2 BHK units (over 2,000 listings), while 4 BHKs remain a rare, niche segment.
* **Zirakpur as the Volume Hub:** VIP Road and Peer Muchalla in Zirakpur dominate overall rental availability, acting as the primary suburban housing hubs.

### 2. Pricing & Location Tiering
* **Chandigarh Commands Premium:** Core Chandigarh sectors (e.g., 36D, 10B) fetch the highest rents, peaking between ₹55,000 and ₹84,000+.
* **Mohali vs. Suburban Value:** Central Mohali (Sectors 79, 66A) commands mid-to-high premium rates, while Kharar and Zirakpur offer affordable volume options.

### 3. Key Property Drivers
* **Room Count Over Square Footage:** Rents correlate much more strongly with the number of bedrooms (0.64) and bathrooms (0.66) than pure square footage (0.47).
* **The Bathroom Premium:** BHK and bathroom counts scale together tightly (0.84 correlation), meaning an extra bathroom directly drives higher rental yield.

### 4. Market Oddities
* **Sector 79 Premium Spike:** While 1 BHKs are cheap, Sector 79 Mohali’s 4 BHKs command a massive premium averaging ₹60,000.
* **Data Anomalies:** Khanpur (Kharar) shows an unrealistically low ₹8,000 average for 4 BHKs, indicating a data entry error or a highly specific outlier.
